🌀 Curly Care Wisdom

Taking care of curly hair is about maintaining moisture and definition.

Don't wash it every day. Rinse with a conditioner only to refresh your curls. Use a deep conditioner or mask weekly to maintain elasticity.

  • Co-wash between shampoos
  • Weekly deep conditioning treatments
  • Protect elasticity for defined curls
